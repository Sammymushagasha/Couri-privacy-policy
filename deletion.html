User Data Deletion Instructions

Last Updated: July 31, 2025

Couri App does not store any personal data from Facebook users. However, if you wish to delete your data associated with Facebook Login, you can do so by:

  1. Go to your Facebook account.
  2. Navigate to Settings & Privacy > Settings > Apps and Websites.
  3. Locate "Couri App" and remove it.
